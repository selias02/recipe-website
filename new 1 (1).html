Recipe of Creamy Carbonara Pasta

A silky creamy carbonara with a taste of happiness Creamy Carbonara Pasta.

This Creamy Carbonara recipe is a plate of heavenly, creamy pasta. Silky spaghetti with crispy pancetta in a super creamy and cheesy sauce. Simply delicious and so easy to make at home.

The recipe below is designed to act as a guide, and not something to be followed prescriptively. Play with your food! Adjust amounts based on your tastes.

Ingredients

Taste of Happiness

  • 250 grams of Elbow Macaroni
  • 3/4 cup of crispy chicken bits
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 packet of all purpose
  • 4 table spoonsgranted cheese
  • 1 sachet of Magic Sarap or Nam2 will do
  • 1/8 teaspoon of Freshly ground pepper

Preparation

  • Boil the pasta according to package direction specified, 10 minutes.
  • Cook chicken bits in a pan to render the fat Strain the chicken bits and set aside in 5 minutes.
  • Saute the garlic in the same pan. Pour the water and Nestle or any all purpose of cream. Stir the cheese and simmer.
  • Strain the spaghetti/pasta and toss in the sauce Transfer on a serving plate and top with a chicken bits.
  • Serve!
